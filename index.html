Delete Your MilkoSys Account

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from MilkoSys, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send us an email at lifecoder4@gmail.com with the subject line Delete My Account.
  2. Include your registered email address or phone number linked to your MilkoSys account.
  3. Once we verify your identity, we will permanently delete your account and associated data within 7 business days.

Data Deletion Policy

The following data will be deleted:

  • Personal details (name, phone, email)
  • Milk collection and billing records
  • Profile information stored in Firebase

Some data (like financial transactions required by law) may be retained for up to 1 year as per compliance requirements.

For any queries, contact us at lifecoder4@gmail.com.
